Metal Roofing

A roof that looks great and lasts a lifetime.

Metal roofing is one of the smartest investments you can make in your home. Beautiful, fire-resistant, low-maintenance, and built to outlast almost anything else on the market.

Why metal

Built to last. Built to look right.

A metal roof is a long-term investment in your home. Installed properly, it can last 50 years or more with minimal maintenance — and it will look better doing it than most alternatives. In Santa Barbara's coastal environment, where salt air, fire risk, and strict design standards all factor in, metal roofing checks every box.

Built to last 50+ years

A well-installed metal roof outlasts asphalt shingles by decades. You may never need to reroof again. Over the long run, it's often the more economical choice when you factor in replacement costs and maintenance.

Class A fire resistance

Metal is non-combustible and carries the highest fire rating available. In a region where wildfire risk is real, a metal roof provides meaningful protection for your home and may reduce your insurance premiums.

Stands up to coastal conditions

Salt air is hard on roofing materials. The right metal roof holds up far better than asphalt or wood in Santa Barbara's coastal environment, with no rotting, warping, or granule loss over time.

Energy efficient

Reflective metal finishes reduce heat gain in summer, lowering your cooling costs. Cool-roof rated products meet California energy code requirements and can make a noticeable difference on your utility bills.

A sustainable choice

Most metal roofing contains significant recycled content and is 100% recyclable at end of life. It's one of the few roofing materials that doesn't end up in a landfill when it's eventually replaced.

Looks as good as it performs

Metal roofing is available in profiles and finishes that suit everything from Spanish Colonial to modern architecture. In Santa Barbara, where design standards are high, a metal roof can meet the most demanding aesthetic requirements.

Roof systems

Four systems to suit any home or style

We fabricate and install four types of metal roofing. Each has its strengths, and we'll help you choose what's right for your home, your budget, and Santa Barbara's design requirements.

01

Standing Seam

The premium standard for residential metal roofing. Concealed fasteners mean a clean, uninterrupted surface with no exposed screws. Exceptionally durable, works well on low slopes, and has a crisp architectural look that performs beautifully through Santa Barbara's design review.

Best for: High-end residential, contemporary and transitional architecture

02

Corrugated Panel

A classic profile that's durable, cost-effective, and well-suited to steeper pitches. Works across a wide range of home styles and is a practical choice where you want the benefits of metal at a more accessible price point.

Best for: A range of home styles, steeper pitches, value-conscious projects

03

Architectural Panel

Custom profiles fabricated to meet specific design requirements. When a standard profile needs to match an existing roofline or satisfy a particular architectural review specification, we build it. This is where our in-house fabrication capability makes a real difference.

Best for: Historic properties, design-reviewed projects, custom rooflines

04

Copper

The finest option for homes where appearance and longevity are both paramount. Copper develops a natural verdigris patina over time that is beautifully suited to Santa Barbara's coastal environment and frequently preferred on historic and high-end properties. A copper roof can last 100 years.

Best for: Historic homes, high-end residential, oceanfront properties
