Materials

We stock what you need

We keep copper, galvanized, and a range of painted metals in stock. Most standard profiles can be fabricated quickly, and we can special-order nearly any metal type or finish you require.

Copper

For gutters, flashing, and architectural details. Copper develops a natural patina that's well-suited to Santa Barbara's coastal environment and high-end design standards.

Galvanized Steel

Durable, cost-effective, and corrosion-resistant. Our standard stock for roofing metals, flashing, and structural sheet metal work.

Painted Metal

Available in a range of colors to match architectural requirements. Ideal when aesthetics matter as much as performance, common in Santa Barbara's design-reviewed projects.

Stainless Steel

Where longevity and appearance both matter. Used for specialty gutters, kitchen and commercial applications, and coastal installations.

Aluminum

Lightweight and corrosion-resistant. A common choice for gutters and flashing in residential applications.

Special Order

If it's not in our standard stock, we can source it. Unusual profiles, specialty finishes, custom gauges, ask us.

Why it matters

Santa Barbara has standards.
We know them.

The City of Santa Barbara and its architectural review process are among the most rigorous in California. Materials, profiles, and finishes that pass elsewhere may not pass here. After 30 years working in this market, we know what's required before the first measurement is taken.

  • Familiarity with Santa Barbara's architectural review and design guidelines, so your project doesn't stall at permitting.
  • Experience matching the materials and profiles the city expects for both residential and commercial work.
  • A local crew that shows up on time, cleans up daily, and understands the expectations of this community.
