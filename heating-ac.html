System types

Every situation has the right solution

Santa Barbara's mild climate and mix of older and newer building stock means one size doesn't fit all. We work with a range of system types to match what makes sense for your home or building.

  • 01

    Ductless Mini-Splits

    Ideal for older homes, additions, or spaces where ductwork isn't practical. Highly efficient, quiet, easy to zone — and no duct losses, which means less energy wasted.

  • 02

    Heat Pumps

    Highly efficient heating and cooling in one system — and a significantly lower carbon footprint than gas. Well-suited to Santa Barbara's mild winters and increasingly required for new construction under California's energy codes.

  • 03

    Central Forced Air

    Traditional ducted systems for whole-home comfort. We design, fabricate the ductwork in our shop, and install the full system.

  • 04

    Commercial HVAC

    Rooftop units, commercial ventilation, and larger-scale systems for offices, restaurants, and retail. We have the certifications and crew for commercial work.
